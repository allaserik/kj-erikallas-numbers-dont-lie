# Simple Habits for Physical Health
1. Exercise regularly, even 15-30 min daily.
2. Drink plenty of water.
3. Eat balanced meals with fruits & veggies.
4. Sleep 7-8 hours consistently.
5. Avoid excessive sugar and junk food.
6. Stretch to keep muscles flexible.
7. Walk or move whenever possible.
8. Take breaks from sitting long hours.
9. Regular check-ups matter.
10. Health is wealth; protect it daily.
